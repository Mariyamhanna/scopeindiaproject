Our Story

Founded in 2007

With over two decades of experience creating extensive internet systems, Suffix E Solutions is a top provider of networking and software services. By concentrating on our clients' primary needs, we provide dependable and useful solutions that are suited to their specifications. From documentation and framework design to coding, testing, and continuing maintenance, we have experience with every stage of the development lifecycle. Our knowledgeable engineers, who have 18 years of experience in a variety of fields, offer detailed direction at every stage of the development process. Our vast knowledge base enables us to provide our clients with accurate and efficient solutions.

Evolution

SCOPE INDIA was launched in 2015 by Suffix E Solutions to address the growing demand for industry-relevant IT training under proficient working professionals. What began as a mission to bridge the gap between education and employment has grown into one of the most recognized names in tech education.

Growth

SCOPE INDIA began its journey in Trivandrum in 2015 and has steadily grown into a multi-location training powerhouse. In 2019, we expanded to Kochi to support more aspiring professionals. In 2021, the Nagercoil branch was proudly launched, and in 2023, we launched our Technopark branch - bringing our high-quality training even closer to the heart of Kerala's IT hub. Each branch reflects our commitment to making world-class IT education accessible to more people.

Present Day Impact

SCOPE INDIA has helped 15,000+ students build successful careers in IT. From fresh graduates to working professionals, our alumni are now placed in reputed companies across India and abroad, thanks to our dedicated placement support and industry-driven training. The team adopts all contemporary technologies from time to time, including Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, DevOps, etc.
