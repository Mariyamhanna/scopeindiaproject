FAQ on SCOPE INDIA

Center for Software, Networking, & Cloud Education

One of India's best Training destinations for Software, Networking, DevOps and Cloud Computing courses with 18 years of Industrial experience. Over 1,000 students find their dream careers each year, and we have assisted more than 15,000 students so far.

What is the full form of SCOPE? The full form of SCOPE is Suffix's Corporate Organisation for Professional Education.
What is SCOPE INDIA? SCOPE INDIA is an organization under Suffix E Solutions, an IT company that provides comprehensive IT support worldwide.
What are the services provided by SCOPE INDIA? SCOPE INDIA provides training in Software, Networking, Software Testing, DevOps, Cloud Servers, and Digital Marketing for both newbies and corporate clients.
What are the types of training modes available? At SCOPE INDIA, we offer both live online and offline classes.
Do you provide placement support? Yes, we provide placement support to all our students until they secure a job.
What are the minimum qualification requirements needed to attending the training? Students who have completed the 12th class are eligible to attend the training.
What is the minimum age limit for attending the training? The training is open to everyone, with no age restrictions.
What is On The Job Training (OJT)? Students who successfully complete the courses get the opportunity to work on live projects alongside experienced developers for a duration of 3 months.
Does SCOPE INDIA provide any placement guarantee? No, we only provide placement support.
Is there any refund option available? No, a refund option is not available as per our company policy.
Who conducts training for the students? The training is conducted by the working professionals of SUFFIX E SOLUTIONS for the students of SCOPE INDIA.
Is SCOPE INDIA an ISO certified organization? Yes, SCOPE INDIA(SUFFIX E SOLUTIONS) is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified Organisation
Does SCOPE INDIA provide a course completion certificate? We provide a COURSE CUM INTERNSHIP certificate.
What are the available class times? Early morning and night batches are available for working professionals, while daytime batches are available for regular students.
What are the fee payment options available in SCOPE INDIA? Both one-time payments and installments are available.
What are the available locations of SCOPE INDIA? The available locations are KOCHI, THAMPANOOR TVM, TECHNOPARK TVM, and NAGERCOIL.
Do you provide training to foreign students? Yes, we provide training to students according to their preferred timings in live online class mode.
Does SCOPE INDIA offer lab facilities to the students? Yes, we provide lab facilities with live support at any time.
What makes SCOPE INDIA unique from other training Institutes? Classes are conducted by working professionals from SUFFIX E SOLUTIONS, including software developers, network engineers, cloud engineers, and DevOps engineers with 3 to 20+ years of experience. The training takes place in a real-world working environment, with live, on-demand doubt-clearing sessions available at any time.
What are the working hours of SCOPE INDIA? The usual working hours are from 9 AM to 6 PM on weekdays and from 9 AM to 1 PM on Saturdays. The office is closed on Sundays.
Does SCOPE INDIA provide any special training programs/workshops to college students? Yes, we offer special training programs and workshops for college students.
What is the duration of the training programs Duration varies depending on the course. Typically, training programs range from 1 to 6 months with additional On-the-Job Training.For more information regarding the duration of courses please feel free to contact our office.
Are there any discounts available for the courses? SCOPE INDIA offers special discounts and offers. Please get in touch with our team to know about any current offers.
What is the teaching methodology at SCOPE INDIA? At SCOPE INDIA we follow a standardized format of teaching with fixed syllabus followed across all five branches. Classes are split into two halves with Theory and Practicals.
Do you provide any study materials? Yes, students receive study materials with discounts.
Can I get recorded sessions if I miss a class? Online classes are recorded and the students will be given a copy of all the recorded classes.
Is there a free trial or demo class available? Yes, demo classes are given to students before they take up a course if they insist.
Can I join multiple courses at once? Yes, students can sign up for multiple courses at different time slots.
